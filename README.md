# lastnextdone-privacy
Privacy Policy for LastNextDone

Last updated: January 2026

LastNextDone is a local-first productivity application. Your privacy is important to us.

Data Collection
LastNextDone does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal data.

All information you enter into the app remains on your device only. The app does not use analytics, tracking, advertising services, or third-party data collection tools.

Data Sharing
No data is shared with the developer or any third parties.

Accounts
LastNextDone does not require you to create an account.

Network Access
LastNextDone does not send personal data over the network.

Children’s Privacy
LastNextDone does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes
If the app’s privacy practices change in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.
